Adventures in Linux

There's no doubt about it, Linux makes my heart beat. Sometime's rather too much until we find a "workaround". There is something about the OS that has always caight my attention. I am not saying that MacOS or WinOS are inferior in anyway, rather each having their own place in the computing world. If you think about it, Windows is the only real odd ball here. MacOS is a based on Unix just as Linux is. Linux/Unix commands work the same on MacOS. This leaves WinOS the odd one of the group. Nonetheless, I adore each of them.
My first run in with Linux was the Debian flavor, from there came Ubuntu then Ubuntu-Mate. I just enjoy the stripped down version of Ubuntu. Initially, it is light-weight but by the time I am development with it it is usually a few gigabits heavier. Absolutely customized to my workflow and nothing else. I typically have a machine that dual boots WinOS and Ubuntu-Mate.
One of these days, I will win the lottery or sell a kidney and get to pickup one of those wicked awesome MacBook Pro's. Better yet! I could get hired by a great employer looking for an entry level/junior developer, that supplies MacBook Pro's to their employees. Fingers crossed Freddy.
